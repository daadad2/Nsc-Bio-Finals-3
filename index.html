Paleozoic Era (541 - 252 million years ago)

The Paleozoic Era was when life really started to get interesting on Earth! It's like when the Crystal Gems first came to Earth - everything was new and exciting. This era saw the first complex life forms, the first plants on land, and even the first animals with backbones!

Cambrian Period

Life during the Cambrian explosion

Life Forms

The Cambrian explosion brought an incredible diversity of life! Trilobites, anomalocaris, and other strange creatures filled the oceans. It was like a big party where everyone was invited!

Geological Features

The supercontinent Rodinia had broken apart, and the continents were scattered. The climate was generally warm, with no ice at the poles. Shallow seas covered much of the land.

Did you know? Almost all major animal groups first appeared during the Cambrian explosion! It's like when Steven first met all the different gems!
